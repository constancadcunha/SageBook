Interview with Marcus

31-year-old designer, intermediate cook. Interview conducted June 5, 2025.

👨‍💻

Marcus, 31

Intermediate Cook

Occupation: Graphic designer

Cooking Frequency: 4-5 times per week

Typical Meals: Experimental dishes, international cuisine

How do you learn new techniques?

🎓
"I watch YouTube chefs, but I hate when they skip steps. Tell me WHY I'm deglazing the pan with wine instead of water."

What's missing in cooking apps?

❌
"They treat recipes like rigid formulas. Cooking is creative! Let me see what happens if I swap paprika for cayenne."
💡

Key Insight

Marcus values understanding food science behind techniques. A "Flavor Lab" feature explaining ingredient interactions would engage him.

How do you feel about community tips?

👥
"Game-changer. Last month someone suggested adding coffee to chili, and now it's my signature dish."
