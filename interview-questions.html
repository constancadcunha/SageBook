Interview Questions

The complete question framework used in our user interviews to understand cooking habits, frustrations, and desired features.

🌿 Interview Question Framework

Cooking Habits & Experience

🍳

How often do you cook at home? What meals do you typically prepare?

🎯

Can you describe a recent cooking success or failure? What happened?

📚

Where do you usually find recipes (apps, books, social media)?

Pain Points

😤

What frustrates you most about following recipes?

🛑

Have you ever abandoned a recipe midway? Why?

🔄

How do you handle missing ingredients or substitutions?

Learning Preferences

🎥

Do you prefer video demos, written steps, or audio guidance?

💡

What would make learning new cooking techniques less intimidating?

👥

How do you feel about community tips on recipes?

App Features

🏆

Would achievements (badges/streaks) motivate you to cook more?

🆘

How useful would a "panic button" for quick fixes during cooking be?

ℹ️

What information do you wish recipes always included?

Design & Tone

🎨

How important is visual style in a cooking app?

👵

Would a cozy, grandmotherly tone make you feel more comfortable?

🌻

How do you feel about the cottagecore aesthetic shown in these examples?
