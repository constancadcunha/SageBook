Interview with Elena

28-year-old marketing associate, returning cook. Interview conducted June 7, 2025.

👩‍💼

Elena, 28

Returning Cook

Occupation: Marketing associate

Cooking Frequency: 3-4 times per week

Typical Meals: Health-focused, simple recipes

How do you meal plan?

📅
"I rotate five 'safe' meals my partner won't reject. If a recipe has more than 5 ingredients, I usually skip it."

What's your cooking dealbreaker?

⏱️
"Time. If I can't find a recipe in three clicks, I'm ordering takeout. And cleanup—no recipes with three pots!"
💡

Key Insight

Elena needs a "Quick Dinner" mode filtering by time, ingredients, and healthiness. Minimal cleanup indicators would help her stay motivated.

Would voice guidance help?

🎙️
"Only if it's interruptible. When my phone rings mid-recipe, I need the app to pause without getting upset."
