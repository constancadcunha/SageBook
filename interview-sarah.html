Interview with Sarah

24-year-old college student, beginner cook. Interview conducted June 3, 2025.

👩‍🎓

Sarah, 24

Beginner Cook

Occupation: College student

Cooking Frequency: 2-3 times per week

Typical Meals: Pasta, scrambled eggs, simple stir-fries

How often do you cook?

🍳
"Maybe twice a week? I mostly make pasta or scrambled eggs. Last week I burned grilled cheese so badly my roommate opened all the windows."

What frustrates you about recipes?

😤
"They say things like 'cook until golden brown' but don't show pictures. Is 2 minutes enough? 5? I need someone to hold my hand!"

How do you feel about cooking apps?

📱
"I downloaded one but deleted it when it asked me to 'julienne carrots' on day one. I don't even own a proper knife!"
💡

Key Insight

Sarah needs ultra-clear visual guidance and reassurance. A "beginner mode" with real-time mistake prevention would address her fear of failure.

Would achievements motivate you?

🏆
"Yes! If I got a 'You Didn't Burn It!' badge, I'd definitely try more recipes."
